## Session Details

### Team Demos to Leadership (60 min)

**Audience:** Invited senior leaders (Head of AI&Data, Head of Tech Consulting, other stakeholders)

Each team: 15 minutes (12 min demo + 3 min Q&A)

**Demo format:**
1. Problem statement (1 min)
2. Live demo (7 min)
3. Architecture + Claude features used (2 min)
4. Lessons learned (2 min)
5. Q&A (3 min)

**Judging criteria (not scored, but discussed):**
- Real-world applicability
- Technical depth
- Claude feature breadth
- Team collaboration
- Demo quality

### Competency Recap (30 min)

- Show pre/post comparison: Day 1 knowledge check vs. end-of-Week-1 assessment
- Highlight key skills gained per domain
- Celebrate specific wins (best lab solutions, creative approaches)

### Trainer Selection (30 min)

- 2-3 participants identified as future co-facilitators
- Selection criteria: technical strength, communication skills, enthusiasm, availability
- Commitment: help plan and co-deliver future cohorts (India, other geos)

### What's Next (45 min)

**Keeping the momentum:**
- Weekly Claude Code office hours (30 min, virtual, first 4 weeks)
- Internal Slack/Teams channel for ongoing questions and sharing
- Monthly "show and tell" — 15-min lightning talks on Claude projects
- Access to facilitators for project support
- Anthropic partnership path: once established, CCA certification for all trained practitioners

**Resources:**
- All curriculum materials remain in the repo
- Lab solutions available after today
- Anthropic documentation, cookbook, and courses bookmarked

### Program Retrospective (30 min)

**Format:** Start / Stop / Continue

- What should we START doing in future cohorts?
- What should we STOP doing?
- What should we CONTINUE doing?

Collect via anonymous form + live discussion.

### Certificates + Close (30 min)

- Completion certificates for all 12 participants
- Recognition of standout contributions
- Group photo (virtual screenshot)
- Final words from PPMD
