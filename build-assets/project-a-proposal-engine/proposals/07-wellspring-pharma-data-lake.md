# Proposal: Wellspring Pharma — Clinical Data Lake & Analytics

**Client:** Wellspring Pharmaceuticals (mid-size pharma, $2B revenue)
**Date:** February 2025
**Value:** $1.6M over 12 months
**Status:** Won
**Team:** Rachel Kim (lead), David Okafor, Aisha Patel, + 5 additional engineers
**Practice:** Data & AI

## Executive Summary

Wellspring's clinical trial data is scattered across 6 systems (EDC, LIMS, CTMS, safety databases, imaging archives, lab systems) with no unified view. Researchers spend 30% of their time locating and reconciling data instead of analyzing it. We propose a HIPAA-compliant clinical data lake on AWS with automated ingestion, harmonization using CDISC standards, and self-service analytics for researchers.

## Approach

**Phase 1 (Months 1-4):** Data lake architecture on AWS (S3 + Glue + Lake Formation). Automated ingestion from 6 source systems. CDISC SDTM data harmonization layer.

**Phase 2 (Months 5-9):** Analytics platform — Snowflake for interactive queries, Tableau dashboards for trial monitoring, and ML models for patient recruitment optimization and adverse event detection.

**Phase 3 (Months 10-12):** AI-powered research assistant using Claude for querying trial data in natural language, generating regulatory submission drafts, and literature review summarization.
