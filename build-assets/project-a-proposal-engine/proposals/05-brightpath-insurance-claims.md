# Proposal: BrightPath Insurance — Claims Processing Automation

**Client:** BrightPath Insurance (property & casualty, 500K policyholders)
**Date:** July 2024
**Value:** $950K over 9 months
**Status:** Won
**Team:** Priya Sharma (lead), Sarah Nakamura, Aisha Patel, + 3 additional engineers
**Practice:** Application Development + Data & AI

## Executive Summary

BrightPath's claims processing is manual and slow — average resolution time is 14 business days, with claims adjusters spending 60% of their time on data entry and document review rather than decision-making. We propose an AI-assisted claims processing platform that automates document intake, extracts claim details, routes claims by complexity, and provides adjusters with AI-generated summaries and recommended actions.

## Approach

**Phase 1 (Months 1-3):** Document intelligence — build OCR + Claude-powered extraction pipeline for claim forms, police reports, medical records, and repair estimates. Structured data output to existing claims database.

**Phase 2 (Months 4-6):** Smart routing and adjuster dashboard — classify claims by complexity (simple/moderate/complex), auto-approve simple claims under $2K, and build adjuster dashboard with AI summaries and action recommendations.

**Phase 3 (Months 7-9):** Integration with policy management system, fraud detection scoring, and customer-facing status portal. Performance optimization and training.

## Key Differentiators

- Aisha Patel mapped the end-to-end claims process and identified that 40% of claims are auto-approvable
- Sarah Nakamura's Claude API expertise for document extraction (90%+ accuracy on structured fields)
- Human-in-the-loop design — AI assists adjusters rather than replacing them, maintaining regulatory compliance
- Priya Sharma's API architecture ensures clean integration with BrightPath's legacy policy system
