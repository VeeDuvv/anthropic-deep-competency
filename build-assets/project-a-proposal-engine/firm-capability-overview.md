# Meridian Consulting — Capability Overview

## About Us

Meridian Consulting is a mid-market technology consulting firm with 1,200 professionals across 8 offices in North America. Founded in 2008, we specialize in digital transformation, cloud modernization, data & AI, and enterprise application development for clients in financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, and retail.

## Core Practices

### Cloud & Infrastructure
Multi-cloud strategy, migration, and managed services across AWS, Azure, and GCP. Certified partnerships with all three hyperscalers. 200+ cloud migrations completed since 2018.

### Data & AI
End-to-end data platform design, analytics engineering, machine learning, and generative AI. Expertise in Snowflake, Databricks, and modern data stack. Growing Anthropic Claude practice for enterprise AI applications.

### Application Development
Full-stack custom development, legacy modernization, API platform design, and DevOps automation. Primarily Python, TypeScript, Java, and Go. Strong Agile/Scrum delivery methodology.

### Digital Strategy
Business process optimization, customer experience design, technology roadmapping, and change management. Cross-functional teams combining business analysts, designers, and engineers.
